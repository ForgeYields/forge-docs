# Power your users’ yield experience

Whether you’re building a wallet, DeFi app, or protocol, ForgeYields gives you direct access to real on-chain yield through automated cross-chain rotation into the strongest incentives and liquidity campaigns, delivering a superior risk–reward profile.

### Why integrate ForgeYields

**Shared revenue model**\
Earn a share of ForgeYields’ fees. Yield becomes a built-in revenue stream for your product.

**Higher risk–reward profile for your users**\
Liquidity automatically shifts toward the highest incentive programs across chains. Positions update as incentives move to maintain strong performance.

**Non-custodial architecture**\
Users always control their assets. Every deposit, batch, rotation, and settlement executes fully on-chain.

**Extreme  transparency**\
Automatic 30-minute reports covering every allocation step: chain-level and protocol-level AUM, all executed transactions, inflow/outflow changes, PnL, updated balances, dust tracking, and a real-time activity feed. Exportable in JSON/CSV.

**Simple, universal integration**\
One-click, chain-agnostic deposits. Asynchronous redeem via a request/claim flow.
