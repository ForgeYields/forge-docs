## Why ForgeYields

Yield aggregators give you safe-list returns. Anonymous farms ask you to take their word for it.

ForgeYields gives you the frontier — with the discipline of structured credit. Every strategy underwritten line by line. Every score published. Hard caps that exclude bad actors before they reach a vault. No *"trust us."*

## What you get

* **Frontier yields** beyond the blue-chip pack — cross-chain, professionally managed.
* **Underwritten by [Hallmark](hallmark/overview.md):** every strategy clears a quantitative bar across protocol, asset, and execution risk before any deposit.
* **Non-custodial:** you hold [fyTokens](basics/fytokens.md), never IOUs.
* **Verifiable:** every score is [published](hallmark/transparency.md), every allocation logged in real-time [Atomic Reports](basics/yield-allocation.md).
* **No lockups:** mint once, auto-compound, redeem on demand.
* **Clear performance:** live [Projected vs Realized yield](basics/performance-tracking.md) tracking.

## Built for

Institutional LPs and sophisticated allocators who want yield above the safe-list — without the wild-west risk. If you read credit memos and care that your risk is published, this is for you.
