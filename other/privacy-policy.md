# Privacy policy

ForgeYields (“we”, “our”, “the Protocol”) is a non-custodial, smart-contract-based yield platform.\
This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how it is used, and your rights regarding that information.

ForgeYields is designed with **data minimization** in mind. We collect as little data as reasonably necessary to operate the website, improve the Protocol, and maintain security.

### **1. Information We Do Not Collect**

ForgeYields **does not** collect or store:

* Private keys or seed phrases
* Custodial access to funds
* Personal identity data (name, address, ID documents, phone number, etc.)
* Bank or payment information
* KYC/AML data (any KYC is handled solely by third-party on-ramp partners)

You always remain in control of your crypto wallet.

***

### **2. Information We May Collect**

We may collect the following non-personal or limited data:

#### **a. On-chain Data**

When interacting with the Protocol, blockchain data becomes publicly accessible by design:

* Wallet addresses
* Transaction hashes
* Balance and contract interaction data

ForgeYields does not store this data off-chain; it exists on the public blockchain.

#### **b. Website Analytics**

To improve the interface and user experience, we may use privacy-friendly analytics (e.g. Plausible, or a similar tool).\
These tools may collect:

* Page views and navigation
* Device type and browser
* Country-level geolocation (not precise location)
* Referring sources

No cookies that track personal behavior or identity are used unless explicitly required.

#### **c. Information You Voluntarily Provide**

If you contact us (email, support forms), we may store:

* Email address or username
* Message content
* Optional screenshots or logs you choose to send

This information is used solely to assist you.

#### **d. Technical Logs**

For security and debugging, servers may briefly log:

* IP addresses (short retention)
* Error logs
* Request metadata

This is standard practice for infrastructure stability.

***

### **3. How We Use the Information**

Information collected may be used to:

* Operate and secure the website
* Improve UI/UX and product performance
* Monitor usage trends (aggregated, anonymous)
* Provide user support
* Prevent abuse or malicious activity
* Diagnose technical issues

We **do not** sell, rent, or trade data with third parties for marketing.

***

### **4. Third-Party Services**

ForgeYields may rely on:

* Hosting services (Hetzner, AWS, DigitalOcean)
* Analytics providers (e.g. Plausible)
* Communication platforms (Telegram)

These providers process limited data as part of their operations and follow their own privacy policies.

***

### **5. Cookies**

ForgeYields may use either:

* **Cookie-less analytics**, or
* Minimal, non-tracking cookies strictly required for functionality.

We do not use advertising cookies or behavioral tracking cookies.

***

### **6. Data Security**

We follow strong security practices:

* Data minimization
* Limited log retention
* Encrypted communication
* No collection of sensitive personal data
* No custody over user wallets or assets

Still, no system is perfectly secure, and you acknowledge these risks when interacting with the Protocol.

***

### **7. User Rights**

You may request:

* Deletion of voluntarily provided contact information
* Information about what data we hold (generally minimal)

Contact: sacha@forgeyields.com

***

### **8. Third-Party Websites & Integrations**

ForgeYields may link to or integrate with external platforms (on-ramps, wallets, block explorers, DeFi protocols).\
These parties have their own privacy policies, and ForgeYields is not responsible for their practices.
